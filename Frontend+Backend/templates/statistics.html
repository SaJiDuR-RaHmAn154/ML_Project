Risk Factors

  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol levels
  • Smoking
  • Diabetes
  • Physical inactivity
  • Unhealthy diet
  • Excessive alcohol consumption

Prevention Tips

  • Regular physical exercise (150 min/week)
  • Maintain a healthy diet
  • Avoid tobacco and limit alcohol
  • Manage stress effectively
  • Regular health checkups
  • Monitor blood pressure
  • Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours)

Take Action Today!

Early detection and lifestyle changes can significantly reduce your risk of heart disease. Stay informed and proactive about your heart health!
