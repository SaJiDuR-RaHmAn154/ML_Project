Common Heart Conditions

Understanding common cardiac conditions helps in early recognition and treatment.

Coronary Artery Disease (CAD)
Description: Narrowing of coronary arteries due to plaque buildup
Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue
Risk Factors: High cholesterol, diabetes, smoking, family history
Treatment: Lifestyle changes, medications, procedures
Arrhythmias
Description: Irregular heart rhythms affecting heart rate
Types: Atrial fibrillation, tachycardia, bradycardia
Symptoms: Palpitations, dizziness, chest discomfort
Management: Monitoring, medications, devices, procedures
Heart Failure
Description: Heart cannot pump blood effectively to meet body's needs
Symptoms: Shortness of breath, swelling, fatigue
Causes: CAD, high blood pressure, diabetes, cardiomyopathy
Treatment: Medications, lifestyle changes, devices
Hypertension
Description: High blood pressure straining heart and arteries
Silent Killer: Often no symptoms until complications occur
Complications: Heart attack, stroke, kidney disease
Control: Diet, exercise, medications, stress management
